Transparency First

Clear Rules. Fair Game.

We believe in complete transparency. Here's exactly how reward eligibility and selection work — no surprises.

🎁
50-Point Reward — Limited to 10 Gifts

Reaching 50 points makes you eligible, not guaranteed. You'll be added to the eligibility list, from which only 10 gifts are awarded in total. The selection method will be communicated to all participants via the portal.

📚
75-Point Reward — All Qualifiers Receive Access

Every participant reaching 75 points will receive course access — no lottery applies. However, access is provided once the course date is scheduled. Your joining link will arrive via email or portal notification in advance.

⚽
100-Point Grand Prize — 3 Winners Selected

Reaching 100 points enters you into the final selection pool. From all eligible 100-point participants, 3 winners are chosen to receive the World Cup 2026 Final ticket and a Coca-Cola fridge.

⚖️
General Eligibility

Open to registered CCEP B2B customers only. One account per business entity. Points are non-transferable. CCEP reserves the right to verify eligibility at all stages.
